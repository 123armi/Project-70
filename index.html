campus life

school canteen

was built in 1996

The school canteen plays a vital role in promoting a culture of healthy eating habits which will be carried through adulthood.School canteen in Army public school Dhaula Kuan is spacious , well ventilated and hygienic with proper staff to serve the students.

primary wing

was established in 1985

The wing provides a pleasing learning environment with the spirit of sharing and caring. The Primary Wing of the school provides the students with opportunities to enjoy learning by participation and develope ability for independent thinking.Group activities are conducted in moderation and in small carefully managed groups. The wing has classes from 1 to 5 with facilities of Computer Labs, Dance room, Western and Classical music as well as Science and Math Lab.

activity block

was established in 1979

Right in the heart of the school lies an exclusive building christened ' The Activity block ' buzzing with activity and dedicated to the teaching and learning of a plethora of Performing Arts namely : all genres of Dance , Indian music, Western music and Percussion instruments to an enormous number of enthusiastic learners.

what's new

  • New School Garden


    The school has a new sprawling 1600 square meter Herbal garden. The garden is a repository of over fifty varieties of plants of ornamental and medicinal value.They are extensively used as teaching aids to introduce students to the joys of Botany.

    Students are regularly taken for a walk through the garden to bring them closer to nature. To add a green touch to their classrooms they are given small plants to grow in small planters.

  • New Clubs


    The school has a new club system which offers different activiteies such as chess, spell-bee, phonetics, drama, acting, gardening, photography, dancing, choir and bird-watching.

    There are specific teachers for this. Student from Class 1 to 12 have at least 2 periods of clubs each week!

    There has been a new sports introduced too- Hand-Football. This has been receiving a positive response from the students so far.
